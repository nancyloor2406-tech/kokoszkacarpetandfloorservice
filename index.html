Frequently Asked Questions

1. How long does hardwood floor refinishing take?

Typically, the process takes 2–5 days depending on the square footage and whether you choose an oil-based or water-based finish to restain wood floor surfaces.

2. What is the best flooring for a high-moisture laundry room?

We highly recommend luxury vinyl plank flooring installation or traditional tile, as these materials are waterproof and highly resistant to chemical spills and heat.

3. Can you perform carpet installation on staircases?

Yes, we specialize in custom carpet installation for stairs, including decorative runners and full-wrap techniques, ensuring both safety and a seamless aesthetic.

4. How often should I schedule professional carpet cleaning?

For most households, we recommend professional carpet cleaning every 12 to 18 months to maintain indoor air quality and fiber integrity.
